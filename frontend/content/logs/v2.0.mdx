This release focuses on **billing**, **languages**, and **trust & transparency**.

## Payments (Creem)

- **Creem** is integrated for purchasing **credits** and **subscriptions**.  
- **Privacy Policy** and **Terms of Service** describe what data is shared with Creem and how payments are processed.  
- Always complete checkout on Creem’s secure pages; we do not store full card numbers on our application servers.

## Languages

- **Spanish (es)** is supported for **documentation** and key **static pages** (privacy, terms) alongside English and Chinese.  
- Fumadocs **i18n** includes `es` so `/docs` and related content can be maintained in three languages.

## Documentation

- **[Documentation](/docs)** now describes product **architecture** (Cloudflare, R2, ECS workers, DeepSeek), **core features** (selection, side-by-side sync, task pipeline), **retention**, and **AGPL** notes with a link to **[zotero-pdf2zh](https://github.com/guaguastandup/zotero-pdf2zh)**.
