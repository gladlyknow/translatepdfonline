## Introduction

**Translate PDF Online** (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the website **www.translatepdfonline.com** and related services (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information when you use the Service.

**Contact:** [hello@translatepdfonline.com](mailto:hello@translatepdfonline.com)

By using the Service, you acknowledge this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

## Information we collect

1. **Account information**  
   Email address, display name, and authentication identifiers you provide when you sign in (e.g. via OAuth or email). Used to create and secure your account and communicate about the Service.

2. **PDF and translation content**  
   PDF files you upload, derived files (e.g. previews, translated PDFs), and technical metadata (page counts, job IDs, timestamps). Used solely to perform translation, previews, billing (e.g. credits per page), and support.

3. **Usage and technical data**  
   Log data, approximate device/browser type, IP address (for security and abuse prevention), and interaction events. Used to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

4. **Cookies and similar technologies**  
   Session and preference cookies (and local storage where used) to keep you signed in and remember settings. You can control cookies through your browser.

5. **Payment-related information**  
   When you purchase credits or subscriptions, **payment processing is performed by our payment partner Creem**. We do not store full card numbers on our servers. We receive from Creem (or your wallet flow) limited data such as transaction status, product identifiers, subscription IDs, and masked payment references as needed for billing and accounting.

## Payment processor (Creem)

We use **Creem** to process payments. Creem collects and processes payment data according to its own policies. We recommend reading Creem’s privacy documentation on their official website: [https://www.creem.io](https://www.creem.io).

We share with Creem only the information necessary to initiate and reconcile payments (e.g. amount, currency, product ID, customer email where required by Creem).

- **Translated files and related objects in object storage** (e.g. Cloudflare R2) are scheduled for **automatic deletion after approximately seven (7) days**, unless we are required to retain them longer by law or you have an open dispute that reasonably requires retention.  
- **Account and billing records** may be retained longer as needed for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, and legal compliance (typically in line with statutory limitation periods).  
- **Logs** are retained for a limited period for security and operations.

Retention practices may evolve; material changes will be reflected in this policy.
