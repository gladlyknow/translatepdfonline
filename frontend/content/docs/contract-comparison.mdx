## What is Contract Comparison?

**Contract Comparison** is an AI-powered tool that analyzes two versions of a document and highlights every difference — added, deleted, or modified text — so you can review changes without manually scanning pages line by line.

It works on contracts, legal agreements, policy drafts, terms of service, and any text-based PDF. Upload an original and a revised copy; the tool returns a structured diff with similarity scoring and categorized change items.

## How it works

1. **Upload** the original document and the modified version (both must be PDF).
2. The system extracts text from both files and runs an AI comparison pass.
3. Results show a **similarity percentage**, a **diff summary** (total items, added, deleted, modified), and structured change details.

Comparison happens server-side. Large documents may take longer; the tool shows progress while the job runs.

## Features

- **Side-by-side comparison** — view original and revised text in parallel.
- **Similarity score** — a percentage indicating how close the two documents are.
- **Categorized diffs** — changes grouped as added, deleted, or modified.
- **Job history** — past comparisons are saved and can be reopened from the History panel.
- **Shareable results** — each comparison gets a unique URL you can share with colleagues.

## Supported formats

- **Input:** PDF (text-based; scanned PDFs should go through OCR first — see [OCR Workbench](/docs/ocr-workbench)).
- **Output:** Interactive diff view in the browser; raw diff data accessible via the comparison result page.

## Notes

- Comparison quality depends on text extraction accuracy. Heavily scanned or image-heavy PDFs may produce incomplete results.
- The tool is optimized for text-level diffs in contracts and legal documents; it does not compare visual layout, signatures, or stamps.
- Uploaded files follow the same [retention policy](/docs#data-security-and-retention) as other documents on the platform.
